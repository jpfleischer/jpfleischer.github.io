SCC: Video Based Machine Learning for Smart Traffic Analysis and Management


PI Name: Dr. Sanjay Ranka

Lab: Modern Artificial Intelligence and Learning Technologies (MALT) Lab

Institution and Department: University of Florida, Department of Computer and Information Science and Engineering

Affiliation Duration: January 2023 - Present

NSF Award: 1922782

Research Focus

This project focuses on developing AI-driven algorithms to improve pedestrian safety by identifying near-miss scenarios through traffic camera footage.

Project Responsibilities

I trained a YOLOv8 AI model to classify objects in detecting dangerous traffic intersection conflicts. I also augmented a full-stack web interface in Vue and Node.js to allow traffic engineers to view historical traffic data, including speed, vehicle throughput, and conflicts.
