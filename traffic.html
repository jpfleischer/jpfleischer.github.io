SCC: Video Based Machine Learning for Smart Traffic Analysis and Management

PI Name: Dr. Sanjay Ranka

Institution and Department: University of Florida, Department of Computer and Information Science and Engineering

Affiliation Duration: January 2023 - Present

NSF Award: 1922782

Research Focus

This project focuses on developing AI-driven algorithms to improve urban traffic management and pedestrian safety. Our goal is to reduce traffic congestion and accidents through predictive modeling and real-time data analysis.

Project Responsibilities

I am responsible for designing and implementing machine learning models that predict traffic patterns based on historical and real-time data. I also work on integrating these models with traffic signal control systems to optimize flow and safety.
